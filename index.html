Keratin ,Hair Transplant

Keratin is a fibrous protein that forms the structural basis of hair, nails, and skin. In hair transplants, the focus is on restoring hair growth through the transplantation of healthy hair follicles, which produce keratin. The procedure involves harvesting follicles from a donor site and implanting them into areas with thinning or no hair. By ensuring these follicles are healthy, the transplanted hair can thrive and generate new strands of keratin-rich hair, leading to a fuller, more natural appearance.
